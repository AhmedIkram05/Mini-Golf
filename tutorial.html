How to Play Mini Golf

1/7
1

Welcome to Mini Golf!

Mini Golf is a simple yet challenging game where your goal is to get the ball into the hole with as few strokes as possible.

Game Overview

2

Aiming and Shooting

To aim, move your cursor in the direction you want to hit the ball. The white line shows your aim direction.

Aiming the ball

Click to hit the ball. The power of your shot is determined by the power slider on the left.

3

Power Control

Use the power slider on the left to control how hard you hit the ball. Higher values will make the ball travel farther.

Power slider

4

Obstacles and Hazards

The course contains various obstacles and hazards:

  • Walls - Bounce off walls to reach the hole
  • Water hazards - Avoid these! Your ball will reset to the starting position with a penalty stroke
  • Sand bunkers - These slow down your ball considerably

Course obstacles

5

Scoring

Each level has a target "par" score. Your goal is to complete the hole in par or better.

  • Birdie: One under par
  • Eagle: Two under par
  • Bogey: One over par

Scoring

6

Power-ups

Some levels contain special power-ups that can help (or challenge) your game:

  • Speed Boost - Increases your ball's speed
  • Perfect Aim - Shows trajectory prediction
  • Ghost Ball - Pass through obstacles temporarily

Power-ups

7

Game Modes

Mini Golf offers several game modes:

  • Single Player - Play through a full course of 10 holes
  • Multiplayer - Challenge friends and take turns
  • Level Editor - Create your own custom courses

You're ready to play! Have fun!
